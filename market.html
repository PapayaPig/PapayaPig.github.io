Advantages of the Vena Vitals Continuous Blood Pressure Monitor

The Vena Vitals Continuous Blood Pressure Monitor provides an innovative solution to the clinical needs with the following advantages:

Usability

A portable wrist-based design and bluetooth data transfer means patients can be completely mobile while wearing the blood pressure monitor. The blood pressure sensor is skin-safe biocompatible and enables repeated bending, flexing, and stretching so that patients can comfortabilty use the device.

Accuracy

The skin conforming wrinkle based sensor in the device provides 600% more surface area than traditional capacitive sensors. As a result, it is capable of direct capacitive pressure sensing which is as accurate as an arterial line.

Cost

Our sensor will market for about $50 USD compared to the Clearsight sensor which costs $350 USD. The cost efficiency of our sensor leads the overall monitoring system being a fraction of the price of our leading competitors, like the Edwards Clearsight System, despite having similar to better performance.
